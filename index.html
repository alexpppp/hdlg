About

Collaborative Farming~

The Hurunui District Landcare Group is a farmer-led, farmer-driven initiative which takes a proactive and collaborative approach to managing, protecting and enhancing the natural and physical resources both on-farm and within North Canterbury's unique catchment communities.

The group has three catchment farm advisors whose role is to support farmers to:

  • Lift farmers understanding of the environmental challenges and opportunities for their property and community
  • Provide expertise to support farmers through changing environmental regulations and assist farmers to develop Farm Environment Plans for their properties.
  • Provide access to Technical Experts to run topic-specific workshops for farmers (e.g. integrating forestry into a farming operation).
  • Support farm ‘clusters’ to address their local issues and help drive change at the community level.

Rebecca was born and bred on a farm in North Canterbury’s Scargill Valley. She understands farming, farmers and the unique climatic and environmental challenges and opportunities the region’s food producers face.
